About Me

My name is Lizzie Lueder, and I’m currently a student in General Assembly’s Web Development Immersive program in Boston.

Prior to GA I was a Finance and Economics major at Boston College. While I enjoyed the logic used in finance and the wide range of applications in economics, I craved a function in which I could use it more creatively.

After graduation I worked on a team that set testing standards for development teams, which involved researching code quality tools, surveying directors of delivery teams, and compiling data. My favorite part of my job was finding ways to automate data entry and speed up processes, which led me to pursuing a track in web development.

I am looking for an opportunity where I can work on a wide breadth of full-stack development projects with a small team of determined developers. I hope to work in an industry in which I can make an impact.
